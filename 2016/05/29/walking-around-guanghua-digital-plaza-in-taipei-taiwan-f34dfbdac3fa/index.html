Walking around Guanghua Digital Plaza in Taipei, Taiwan

Guanghua digital plaza (光華商場) is the Taipei electronic market which is not far from Zhongxiao Xinsheng station (忠孝新生站). You can visit from 10:00 am to 9:00 pm.

Traveling to the plaza is easy by using exit 1 near Zhongxiao Elementary School (忠孝国小) and following the signs which was written “Guanghua Digital Plaza” until you found the market.

When I arrived, I walked around each floor. I found that each floor sells electronic devices such as phones, tablets, notebooks which is local brands like ASUS, MSI, Gigabyte. In addition to selling products, There are many service centers you can send your devices to repair.

After walking around, I compared between the market and Pantip Plaza which is the Thai electronic market.

I found that the market is larger and better than the Thai market. However, I did not found any difference between the costs of Taiwan and Thai electronic devices.

If someone ask me to recommend Taiwan electronic devices, I tell them to buy the devices which are not sold or sold in Thailand but more expensive.
