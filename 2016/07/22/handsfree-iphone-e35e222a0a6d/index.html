The Handsfree iPhone

The days before I saw the iPhone Siri advertisement, and I tried using by enabling Siri usage and Always On Siri. Then, I set to activate Siri by saying “หวัดดีสิริ” (or “Hello Siri” in English).

After activating Siri, I tried using for a while, and I found that it made my life easier. Instead of opening iPhone to swipe and touch, it could do many things I wanted by calling “Hello Siri” then saying any commands I request to do.

Other than Siri, I could type any text by touching microphone on the keyboard and saying anything I wanted instead of using my finger to move and touch on keyboard to type. This function is called “Dictation”.

Both features could make me use my finger less than before. However, I am addicted using finger to type, and I feel too shy to use outside my bedroom according to this article.

As a result, I do not use Siri and Dictation anymore.
