After upgrading, I impressed many things I like such as universal apps: Facebook, Messenger, Microsoft Office etc and responsiveness. I used every day, until I try other operating systems like Linux.

I decided to remove Windows 10 and install Linux. Linux on my tablet is not as good as Windows 10, so I removed Linux to install Windows 10 again. I used for about two or three months. Then, I could not use Wi-Fi, although I tried reinstalling Windows 10 for about three or four times.
